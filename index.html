Agroautomated

New system agriculture typically refers to innovative and sustainable approaches to farming that aim to address the challenges of modern agriculture, such as environmental sustainability, food security, and resource efficiency. These new agricultural systems often incorporate advanced technologies and practices to optimize crop production while minimizing negative environmental impacts.

We Make Your Business Smarter with New Agricultural Methods

A mobile app and a compact device that supports smart agriculture solutions that can reduce insufficient use of irrigation water and inefficient soil moisture management issues. We decided to create an Arduino device that has: a soil moisture level sensor, a water level sensor for the water tank, and a canal to establish efficient irrigation of the soil and with this irrigation, added parameters that are coming from the device, automations that the device provides, agriculture can be enriching.
