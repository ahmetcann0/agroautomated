New system agriculture typically refers to innovative and sustainable approaches to farming that aim to address the challenges of modern agriculture, such as environmental sustainability, food security, and resource efficiency. These new agricultural systems often incorporate advanced technologies and practices to optimize crop production while minimizing negative environmental impacts.

We Make Your Business Smarter with New Agricultural Methods

Agricultural Automation is a method developed to increase productivity through the use of technology in the agricultural sector. These systems automate farming processes such as irrigation, fertilization, soil analysis and harvesting. Farming automation helps farmers use resources more effectively while reducing workload. It also contributes to making agriculture more sustainable thanks to data analytics and remote monitoring. Therefore, agricultural automation plays an important role for the future of modern agriculture.
